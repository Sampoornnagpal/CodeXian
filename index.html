HOW IT WORKS

6 Phases. Zero Manual Work.

01

Catalog

AI generates a structured BOM with components, nets, power rails, and reference designators from your intent.

02

Map

Every component is mapped to exact KiCad footprint libraries. 3-tier lookup with AI fallback for unknowns.

03

Place

Components placed intelligently — signal flow, thermal management, decoupling proximity, board area optimization.

04

Route

Manhattan routing with net-class-aware widths. Power traces 0.5mm, signal traces 0.25mm. Star topology for multi-pin nets.

05

Compile

All phases merged into a single master build script that executes inside KiCad — one click to render the complete PCB.

06

Validate

Shadow Engineer runs 15+ checks (ERC, DRC, SI/PI). Auto-fix engine repairs violations and re-validates.
